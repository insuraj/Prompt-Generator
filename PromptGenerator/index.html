How to Use

1. Enter the statement you want your prompt to be based on in the input box.

2. Click "Send" or press "Enter" to submit.

3. Based on your input the AI will generate:

  • Revised Prompt: A clear, concise version of your input.
  • Suggestions: Details to include to improve the prompt.
  • Questions: Relevant questions for additional information needed.

4. If you are not satisfied with the output you can improve your prompt further by giving addtional information from the Suggestions and Questions

5. Once you are satisfied with the Generated Prompt You are good to go.

Where to Paste

Once the prompt is generated, you can use it in your respective AI applications to achieve the desired outcomes.
